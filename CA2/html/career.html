Ethical Hacker

Career 1

Ethical hackers uses their knowledge to secure and improve the technological part of an organization. They are crucial in providing the service to these organizations by hunting for vulnerabilities that can potentially lead to a security breach. An ethical hacker also reports identified vulnerabilities to the oganization and fixes the bugs.

Fullstack Developer

Career 2

A full stack developer needs to be familiar with both the front and back ends of websites. They develop both client and server softwares. In addition to having to master CSS and HTML, they also need to have the knowledge on how to program a browser using softwares such as Javascript, Angular and program the server using Python and PHP.

Cybersecurity Analyst

Career 3

A cybersecurity analyst protects a company's software, hardware and networks from potential cybercriminals. Their primary role is to understand the IT infrastructure of the company in detail and to monitor the infrastructure at all times to evaluate threats that could potentially cause damange to the company's network.
