Common ICT
Programme (S32)

Course 1

Common ICT Programmes (DCITP) is a semester long programme designed by SP to introduce students to the vast landscape in the IT world. DCITP helps students to gain insight into the various occupations, job roles, career pathways and employment opportunities in this sector. By joining DCITP, students will be able to achieve a strong foundation in programming and IT fundamentals which will enable them to better choose the course that they wish to pursue.

Information Technology (S69)

Course 2

Information Technology (IT) is the main driving force behind many digital transformations that we use and see daily. IT professionals are in high demand all over the world. This course mainly focuses on Software Development, User Experience (UX) and Immersive Simulation which will create valuable opportunities for students. Leading industry companies like Microsoft, GovTech, Singtel, UOB and more also offers internship opprtunities and project collaborations.

Infocomm security Management (S54)

Course 3

As the world becomes more digitalised, getting hacked is going to be much more common than before. Our reliance on the internet to manage and store sensitive information increases everyday and hence, we will need to prepare ourselves more to keep cybercriminals away. By joining this cornse, students will get a competitive edge for a boost in their studies and career. The modules will also cover both the offensive and defensive ends, including investigative skills. There are also many certifications offered by the industry for students to boost their learning and get recognized.

AI and Analytics (S30)

Course 4

Did you know that people interact with a form of Artificial Intelligence (AI) everyday? From Google Home to Siri, data analytics and AI are the magic behind these devices and programmes which makes it possible for them to respond to us in a human-like manner. If you want to be a part of the revolution, this course is for you as AI technology is transforming every industry such as manufacturing, healthcare and etc. This course also offers professional certifications from companies such as Microsoft and AI Singapore which gives students a chance to gain industry recognition.
